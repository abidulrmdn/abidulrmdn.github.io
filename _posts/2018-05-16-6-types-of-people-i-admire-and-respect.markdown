## 6 Types Of People I Admire And Respect

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/2560/1*yBiwJ6rpUEtFhLoZwVeO8Q.jpeg)

Since I started walking, seeing and realising what’s around me in such a fast paced life, I developed a flagged people catalogue in my brain, or I’d rather say a personality types catalogue that I had an utmost respect and admiration for.

## P1. Observant and Optimistic

*You’ll notice I refer to (he/she that I’ve met before) in each section as ****X***

X looks at things always in a new perspective, which I found to be very helpful, because the moment you start getting used to things around you wether it was environment, a book or code, you start losing details. of course being observant alone won’t work, you need not to be very realistic, cause most of whats around isn’t perfect, you need to observe all and pick whats interesting and always be excited about it.

## P2. Joyful and Hard-working

*You’ll notice I refer to (he/she that I’ve met before) in each section as ****X***

X is always happy, or at least shows it. when **X** hangouts with people he/she brings cheer around. Those who know **X**, admire **X**’s attitude, and they always invite **X** to outings and fun gatherings. Of course, the **X** i admire and respect usually comes as a hardworker as well. Just being fun feels like a loser to me.

## P3. Quick-witted but Mannered

*You’ll notice I refer to (he/she that I’ve met before) in each section as ****X***

X thinks fast and respond fast, always surprise those around **X** with clever out of the box solutions. people always consult **X** and try to gain **X**’s approval without realizing. what I most respect about **X** is that **X **is humble and not arrogant, even though **X **knows it all, **X **will still ask those around him.

## P4. Dedicated and Responsible.

*You’ll notice I refer to (he/she that I’ve met before) in each section as ****X***

X** **takes responsibilities for his actions and even for those around **X **that **X **cares about, one other thing is that once **X **puts his mind onto something, will never rest till accomplished. of course if **X **was just** **dedicated, **X **would just be iron-headed.

## P5. Super confident and respectful

*You’ll notice I refer to (he/she that I’ve met before) in each section as ****X***

X is super confident, which makes people around **X **to want **X **to lead them without realising. **X** isn’t selfish and arrogant though, and he bases his confidence on what **X** know. **X** knows when to say I need to look that up first and then **X** will get back to you. **X** doesn’t answer questions not in **X**’s knowledge, cause **X **knows that that attitude isn’t nice, and people don’t respect that.

## P6. Patient, consistent and actionable

*You’ll notice I refer to (he/she that I’ve met before) in each section as ****X***

X is very patient and knows how to control **X**’s attitude when stress and pressure comes in. of course **X** knows that things must be done in those situations, anf **X** knows how to handle situations like those. **X** knows exactly how to act on each, without setting anyone off. 
Patience alone is annoying without action.
