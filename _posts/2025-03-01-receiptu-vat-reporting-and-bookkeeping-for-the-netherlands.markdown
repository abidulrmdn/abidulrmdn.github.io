## What Is BTW and the Btw-Aangifte?

**BTW** is the Dutch term for VAT (value-added tax). In the Netherlands, **omzetbelasting** is effectively the same thing: a tax on the value you add when you sell goods or services. If you’re registered for VAT, you must report it regularly.

That’s where the **btw-aangifte** comes in. It’s the official VAT return: the form or declaration you file with the Dutch Tax and Customs Administration, the **Belastingdienst**. In the Netherlands, most businesses submit this **quarterly** via the Belastingdienst portal at belastingdienst.nl. Getting the numbers right means having your receipts and invoices in order and knowing your taxable turnover, deductible VAT, and what you owe or are owed.
