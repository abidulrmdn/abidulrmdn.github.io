## How are clouds formed

You know when it's too cold sometimes that you can see your breath when you’re breathing while freezing. the same thing happens up there. when the humid hot jet exhaust mixes with the atmosphere up there in the sky, that vapor coming out from the jet is condensed and will freeze if conditions are met and this process of jet vapor being frozen causes the clouds (in simple words).

Now those clouds have different shapes and styles depends on how are they created. whether it was natural by the spread vapor in the air that freezes and causes the long-widely spread clouds or sometimes as we said because of jets and jets clouds cause many different styles as well depending on the jets and how they function.

## X in the sky

After discussing how clouds are made and the theories behind jet clouds, Its time for us to reach a conclusion.

Air traffic is higher than some might think, if you look at the picture below you can see that its too crowded up there and the propability of 2 planes routes intersecting at a certain point is very high.
